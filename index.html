About QuickTask Ireland

Home Jobs Login Sign Up About Us

Who We Are

QuickTask Ireland is a platform built to connect people across Ireland who need short-term jobs completed — whether big or small — with skilled or unskilled workers who are ready to help. From changing sinks to fixing tyres, cutting grass to professional fence installation, our site gives people a place to find help quickly and reliably.

What We Offer

Our website allows anyone to post jobs on short notice. Some tasks may require specific qualifications and experience, while others are simple jobs that anyone can do. This flexibility means that people with a wide variety of skills — from professionals to casual helpers — can find opportunities and connect with those who need work done quickly.

Meet the Founders

Cianan Power

Cianan is one of the driving forces behind QuickTask Ireland. With a strong vision to create a trusted online space where people can both find work and get help with everyday tasks, Cianan focuses on innovation, accessibility, and user experience.

Adam Cooney

Adam brings creativity and commitment to the team. He is passionate about building a platform that works for everyone — from homeowners needing quick assistance to professionals looking to expand their client base.
